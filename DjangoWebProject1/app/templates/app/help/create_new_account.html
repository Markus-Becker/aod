Step-by-step registration instructions:

  1. Go to the Registration page
  2. Enter your first and last name, a unique username and a valid email account
  3. Enter your personal password and then re-enter it
  4. Optionally, enter your mobile number for notification purposes
  5. Define the geolocation information (e.g. country)
  6. Advertise your roles (buyer, seller or/and carer)
  7. Select the channels of your interest
  8. Answer to the simple question
  9. Since you have read the "AoD Marketplace User Agreement" and "Privacy policy", submit your registration request
  10. Check your email account and follow the instructions step-by-step to activate it
