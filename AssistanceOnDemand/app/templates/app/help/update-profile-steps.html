Do you want to update your profile?


{% block list %}

Instructions:

  1. Navigate on the top menu, click on your username and select the Profile link
  2. The AoD platform provides the following capabilities:
    • Update your cover image. Optionally, you can update your cover image since you click on Add cover button and select an image of your interest.
    • Modify your Personal information. Go to Personal information box and click on Edit button to alter the view in edit mode. You can fill or correct any field related to your first/last name and your geolocation options. Optionally, you could upload or change your profile logo. To store the changes, just click on Save button. To cancel your changes, click on Cancel button
    • Modify your Contact information. Go to Contact information box and click on Edit button to alter the view in edit mode. You can fill or correct your email account and your mobile phone number. To store the changes, just click on Save button. To cancel your changes, click on Cancel button
    • Modify your Platform settings. Go to Platform settings box and click on Edit button to alter the view in edit mode. You can fill or correct your IT experience, your desired roles in platform (e.g. service consumer, service provider or carer). By default, any provider receives notification for crowd-funding procedures while any consumer has to specify if he/she want to receive and participate in these procedures. Moreover, you can update the channels of your interest (service categories) from the drop down list. To store the changes, just click on Save button. To cancel your changes, click on Cancel button
    • Modify your Bank settings. Under development.
